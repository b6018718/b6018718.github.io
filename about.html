G For Grandetta is a turn-based role playing game, a genre notable for its intense stories with highly strategic battles, and G For Grandetta is no different!

Choose a class and then give your hero a name to begin the adventure!

The game begins when you, the hero, awake from slumber to find your once bustling village is completely devoid of life. The exception being a lone stray dog standing atop the grave of your late grandma...
